NOTE: Fengyun-1C (FY-1C) is a polar orbiting weather satellite. It has a mass of 958 kilograms. Fengyun-1C was colaunched with Shijian-5 on the first launch of a Long March 4B. President Clinton gave Fengyun-1C a waiver in 1996 for launch on a Chinese rocket.
